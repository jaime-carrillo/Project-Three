Emergency Department Visits

Research suggests that nearly 30% of ED visits “could be managed in physician offices, clinics and urgent care centers” (Weinick, Burns, & Mehro, 2010)

The Problem

Our question and approach
Question : What are the features that lead to a “frequent flyer”? Based on some research in our previous project, we hypnotized that some of the features stem from age, race, gender, income, insurance, social support, convenience, cost, and access to care.

Approach #1: Can we use patient data to train a model to predict who's most liekly to visit the ER for primary care services?

Approach #2: Can we use hospital and facility data to train a model to identify hospitals that have a higher average of ER visits not resulting in admission?
